Pet Rock the tortoise.

Pet Rock was my first pet ever. i got him when i was four years old.

Naming

Pet Rock got his name immediately i saw him because at first he was hiding inside his shell and looked like a rock.

Playing

After a while we became best friends and played together all the time.

Our favorite game was 'roll over and back'

Food

I fed him raw cabbage and other vegetables. he was very hapy to be my friend.

Good bye

a few years later he was taken away to start a new life in the wild with a new friend of his own kind. i was very sad.
